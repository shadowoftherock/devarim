## The Seven Noahide Laws

The **Noahide Laws** (also called the **Seven Laws of Noah**) are a set of moral laws that, according to Jewish tradition, were given by HaShem to **all humanity** (through Noah) and are binding on **non-Jews**.


1. **Do not worship idols**
   – Prohibits idolatry and the worship of false gods.

2. **Do not curse or blaspheme HaShem**
   – Requires respect and reverence for HaShem.

3. **Do not murder**
   – Upholds the sanctity of human life.

4. **Do not commit sexual immorality**
   – Prohibits adultery, incest, bestiality, and other forbidden sexual relations.

5. **Do not steal**
   – Forbids theft, robbery, fraud, and dishonesty.

6. **Do not eat flesh torn from a living animal**
   – Teaches compassion for animals and respect for life.

7. **Establish courts of justice**
   – Requires societies to create fair legal systems to uphold these laws.

## The 30 Noahide Commandments (Traditional Enumeration)

Below is a **traditional list of 30 Noahide commandments** as taught by **some rabbinic authorities** (notably attributed to interpretations found in the **Talmud**, **Rambam (Maimonides)**, and later commentators such as the **Meiri** and **R. Isaac of Dampierre**).

⚠️ Important note: **There is no single universally binding list of “the 30”**—different sages enumerate them slightly differently. This is a **commonly taught composite list** used in Noahide study circles.

---


### A. Faith & God (related to Idolatry & Blasphemy)

1. Believe in **one God**
2. Do not worship idols
3. Do not create idols for worship
4. Do not pray to idols
5. Do not sacrifice to idols
6. Do not bow to idols
7. Do not curse or blaspheme HaShem
8. Do not profane HaShem’s Name

---

### B. Human Life & Dignity (related to Murder)

9. Do not murder
10. Do not commit suicide
11. Do not cause bodily harm
12. Do not kidnap
13. Do not sacrifice humans
14. Do not endanger human life

---

### C. Sexual Morality

15. Do not commit adultery
16. Do not engage in incest
17. Do not commit rape
18. Do not engage in bestiality
19. Do not engage in forbidden same-sex relations (traditional Noahide view)
20. Do not engage in sexual exploitation

---

### D. Property & Economic Justice (related to Theft)

21. Do not steal
22. Do not rob or burglarize
23. Do not commit fraud or deception
24. Do not withhold wages
25. Do not cheat in measurements or business

---

### E. Justice & Social Order (Courts of Law)

26. Establish courts of justice
27. Appoint honest judges
28. Do not accept or give bribes
29. Enforce laws fairly and equally

---

### F. Compassion for Living Creatures

30. Do not eat flesh taken from a living animal
    *(often expanded to include avoiding unnecessary cruelty to animals)*

---
